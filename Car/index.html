About Car

Car is the final proejct created for CS 250 Software Engineering class. Car is a toy RC car that has been modifed to be controlled through a simple web app. The platform is built on a toy Dodge 2016 Challenger HellCat.

The car features a live stream that will allow you to see what the car sees. The car can be controlled by arrow keys and WASD.

The system is built on a Raspberry Pi 2 and uses a H bridge motor driver to control the motores. We have built a custom circut than has been soldered on. The system also is highly adaptable to other vehicles with similar controls.
